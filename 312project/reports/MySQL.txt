MySQLWhat does this technology (library/framework/service) accomplish for you?MySQL creates a database for storing and managing submitted data in our website, such as user’s post content and meta-information. (content ID, filename, number of vote) How does this technology accomplish what it does?Each time the users trying to get or submit content, our website will send a specific SQL statements on MySQL, and the database will respond with the requested information and the information will appear on the website.What license(s) or terms of service apply to this technology?MySQL is free and open-source software under the terms of the GNU General Public License, and is also available under a variety of proprietary licenses. Copyright (c) 1997, 2020, Oracle and/or its affiliates.
